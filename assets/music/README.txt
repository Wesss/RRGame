Music assets are organized as follows:

assets/music/<artist name>/<artist name>.att       --- contains 3 lines of text of:
	the URL of this user's homepage to which to give attribution
	the lisence of the track
	the URL to the lisence of the track
assets/music/<artist name>/<track name>.ogg        --- the music tracks by this artist
assets/music/<artist name>/unused/<track name>.mp3 --- unused music by this artist
